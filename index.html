How It Works
From what you know to what you need
Most people searching for a bearing know the application or shaft size. Not always the part number. MYCELA bridges that gap.
Step 01
Describe what you need
Type in plain language. Part number, shaft size, application, sealing type. Any of these work.
Step 02
AI reads your intent
The search engine maps your query against real technical specifications, not just keywords.
Step 03
Review full spec sheets
Load ratings, RPM limits, sealing type, mass. Complete data so you can make a confident call.
Step 04
Find cross-references
One click shows equivalent parts across brands. Switch from NTN to SKF without any rework.
